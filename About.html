Hobbies

When I have free time, I tend to spend most of it playing video games. At the moment, the game I mainly play is SMITE, but throw in some other games like Rocket League, Valorant, and Call of Duty at times. I have been a gamer my entire life, and started on the Nintendo 64 and Gameboy with Pokemon.

I am currently a member of the Michigan State Paintball Team. I originally started playing paintball in 2016 for fun. I generally would only play once or twice a year, but joined MSU's paintball team my sophmore year (2018) and have been loving every second of it. We hold practices every-other week, with conditioning on the weeks we do not have practice. We are a speedball team, meaning our games are extremely fast-paced, and generally over within two minutes! We play these speedball games in tournaments, and the teams are either a five-verse-five, or three-verse-three. Imagine ten people on a 40-50 yard field firing fully automatic paintball guns. It gets very hectic right away, and that is why the rounds usually last less than two minutes.
